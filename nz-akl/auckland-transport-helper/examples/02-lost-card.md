# Example 2 — Lost HOP card, transfer balance and concession

## Input
> Lost my HOP card on the train this morning. Card ends 8732. Registered to me. Approximate $24 balance. Tertiary concession registered last September. Need a replacement.

## Output
```
DRAFT — AT HOP lost card report
-------------------------------
AT HOP card ending: 8732
Registered to: <NAME>
Approximate last use: 2026-05-20, train (Western line)
Approximate balance: $24
Concession on card: Tertiary (registered 2025-09)

Card was lost on 2026-05-20. Please disable the card immediately
to prevent unauthorised use, and reissue a replacement. Transfer
the remaining balance and Tertiary concession to the new card.

<NAME>
<DAYTIME PHONE>
<DELIVERY ADDRESS>
```

```
What to do next
---------------
1. Sign in to AT HOP online: https://at.govt.nz/bus-train-ferry/at-hop-card/
2. Lost / stolen card → "Report card lost".
3. The site will disable the card immediately.
4. Replacement card mails to the registered address (about 3 working days).
5. Until the new card arrives, top up paper / contactless options on
   the bus, or use AT Mobile with QR (where supported on your route).
```
