# Changelog

## [1.0.1] — 2026-05-25

### Fixed
- Corrected the photo limit from "1–10 photos" to "1–20 photos" (Trade Me allows up to 20 images per Marketplace listing) in SKILL.md and README.md.
- Replaced the dead restricted-items help link with the current Banned and Restricted page (https://www.trademe.co.nz/c/trust-safety/banned-and-restricted).
- Fixed the "Sucessful Trade fees" typo and updated the fee note: from 10 March 2026 Trade Me dropped the 7.9% success fee for casual sellers on Marketplace (general items list free; buyers pay a small service fee), while in-trade sellers still pay success fees.
- Updated the Buyer Protection note: cover applies when the buyer pays via Ping or Afterpay, up to $5,000; corrected the description of Ping from "escrow-style" to Trade Me's in-app card payment system.

## [1.0.0] — 2026-05-20

### Added
- Initial release.
- Drafts Trade Me listing title (≤ 80 chars), category suggestion, condition, description, postage note.
- Extracts visible specs and measurements from photos when possible.
- NZ-direct tone; refuses clickbait and SHOUTY CAPS.
- Refuses to auto-list, set hard prices, or misrepresent condition.
- Zero network permissions.
