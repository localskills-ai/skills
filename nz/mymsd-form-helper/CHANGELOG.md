# Changelog

## [1.0.1] — 2026-05-25

### Fixed
- **Product name corrected: "Recoverable Assistance Programme" → "Recoverable Assistance Payment"** (Work and Income's current product name) in SKILL.md and README.md.
- **Removed an inaccurate decision-timeframe claim.** SKILL.md and example 01 said "Most decisions issue within 5 working days" — Work and Income does not state this. Replaced with the accurate guidance: applicants have 20 working days from first contact to complete the application, and first payment is usually within 2–3 weeks of applying (depending on circumstances and any stand-down).
- **Dead appeals link replaced.** The README's Social Security Appeal Authority link pointed to a 404 page on msd.govt.nz. The Authority is run by the Ministry of Justice; updated to `justice.govt.nz/tribunals/social-security-appeal-authority/` and noted the decision is first reviewed by MSD's Benefits Review Committee.

## [1.0.0] — 2026-05-20

### Added
- Initial release.
- Covers Jobseeker Support, Sole Parent Support, Supported Living Payment, Accommodation Supplement, Special Needs Grants, Recoverable Assistance loans, reviews, change-of-circumstances.
- Produces document checklist tailored to form + circumstances.
- Drafts open-text answers in factual MSD-appropriate register.
- Refuses misrepresentation; refuses eligibility judgements; refuses to file.
- Refers to Community Law and MoneyTalks for the things outside its scope.
- Marked safetyLevel: medium given the stakes.
