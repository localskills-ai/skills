# Example 1 — Recent redundancy, applying for Jobseeker + Accommodation Supplement

## Input
> I was made redundant from Air New Zealand on 8 May 2026. I rent in Mt Eden, Auckland, $480/week, just me on the lease. Single, no kids. Savings around $4k. Need help applying for Jobseeker.

## Output
```
What to gather before opening the form
--------------------------------------
- IRD number
- MyMSD client number (if you've claimed before) or driver's licence
- Redundancy letter from Air New Zealand
- Last 3 payslips
- Last 3 months of bank statements (all accounts)
- Tenancy agreement (current one — the one with the bond receipt)
- A list of jobs you've applied for in the last 7 days (start one
  today; MSD checks this from week one)
```

```
Draft — "Why are you applying?"
-------------------------------
I was made redundant from Air New Zealand on 8 May 2026. I am
actively job-hunting in airline operations / customer service in
Auckland and have applied for 6 roles since 9 May. I'm applying for
Jobseeker Support to cover essential costs while I find new work,
and Accommodation Supplement to help with rent of $480/week.
```

```
Draft — "Tell us about your situation"
--------------------------------------
I live alone in Mt Eden, Auckland, in a rented flat (sole-name
lease, $480/week). My most recent role was at Air New Zealand
earning $1,180/week gross. Since redundancy my only income has been
my final-pay payout. I have approximately $4,000 in savings. My
weekly outgoings are $480 rent, ~$60 utilities (power + internet),
and ~$120 food.
```

```
What to do next
---------------
1. Sign in to MyMSD: https://my.msd.govt.nz/
2. Apply → Jobseeker Support → tick Accommodation Supplement on the
   same flow.
3. Have the documents above ready.
4. Use the drafts above as starting points — edit before submitting
   (especially the job-search list).
5. Submit. You have 20 working days from first contact to finish the
   application; first payment is usually within 2–3 weeks of applying.
6. Watch your MyMSD inbox — respond same-day to any "more
   information" requests.
```
