# Example 2 — Refusal, solar export on bill

## Input
> Here's my Mercury bill. I've got 5kW of solar on the roof so I see export credits. Should I switch?

## Output
```
This skill doesn't analyse solar-export accounts. Solar economics depend
heavily on:
  - The buy-back rate your retailer offers per exported kWh.
  - Time-of-use of your solar generation (sunny midday vs your usage curve).
  - Whether you have battery storage.

A switch that looks cheaper on consumption can be worse overall if the
solar buy-back rate drops.

For solar-specific analysis use Powerswitch's solar buy-back rates:
https://www.powerswitch.org.nz/solar-rates

Happy to compare consumption-only plans if you want a rough sanity
check, but please don't switch on the strength of that alone for a
solar setup.
```
